Exercise 8 Description:
Until now, you have used your phone to read the QR codes. In this exercise, you must use your laptop with a Camera (internal or external) to read QR codes. Your script must do the following:
- Use a camera to read QR codes.
- The data within the QR code must be read and exported to a file.
- The name of the file must reflect the date and time at that moment.
- Make several QR codes with the script from Exercise 7 to test.
